We make food from the heart

Kasama translates to “together,” speaking to the husband and wife team - Tim Flores and Genie Kwon, and the Filipino food culture. It’s about gathering, sharing, and connecting.

An ambient Dining experience

At our modern Filipino restaurant, we’ve created an ambient dining experience that’s a true celebration of Filipino culture, flavors, and hospitality. Join us and savor the best of the Philippines in a setting that combines the best of tradition and modernity.
